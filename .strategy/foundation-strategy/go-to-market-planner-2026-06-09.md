## Phase 1 (Months 0–9): Land the first 8–10 SME clients

**Objective**: Cash-positive + 3–5 case studies. North-star: **paying clients**.

### Channels (ranked by ROI for a bootstrapper)
1. **Founder's warm network + LinkedIn** — *the fastest path to first revenue.* Phil personally knows or is one degree from UK SME owners. Direct outreach + LinkedIn content sharing the framework and his operator story. **This, not SEO, generates the first clients.**
2. **Referral asks** — every conversation (client or not) ends with "who else do you know wrestling with this?" Accountants, fractional FDs, business advisors = referral partners serving the same owners.
3. **SEO/content (already built)** — the *compounding* channel; pays off over months, not weeks. Keep publishing per the cluster plan. It's the long-game moat, not the Month-1 lead source.
4. **Targeted local/regional** — UK business networks (BNI, chambers, regional founder groups) where "UK-native operating system" resonates and US import-fatigue is highest.

### The funnel
- **Top**: Blog post / LinkedIn / referral →
- **Lead magnet**: A free "90-Day Planning Guide" or a Traction-style short book (build this — it's the #1 missing funnel asset) →
- **Paid entry**: **Alignment Diagnostic £500–950** (qualifies + earns) →
- **Core**: 90-Day Kickstart → Quarterly/Annual Operating Partner.

### Founder sales rhythm (the discipline that's missing)
- Block **2–3 hours/day for outbound + sales** before any "building."
- Target **5 quality conversations/week** → at ~20–30% close, that's the path to 8–10 clients in 6 months.
- Track in a simple CRM (even a spreadsheet): conversations → diagnostics → proposals → closed.

### Phase 1 fixes to ship on the site (this week)
1. **Remove the fabricated "100+ companies / 40%" stats** (legal + trust risk) → replace with founder-credibility + method framing.
2. **Add a pricing anchor**: "EOS clients pay £36K+/yr. 1:90 Align from £6,000." + the paid Diagnostic as the entry CTA.
3. **Add a lead magnet capture** (the 90-Day guide) to convert SEO traffic into a contactable list — right now traffic leaks away uncaptured.
4. **Strengthen the founder story page** — it's the highest-converting asset in a trust sale.

## Phase 2 (Months 9–24): Build the certified-coach network

**Objective**: Break the founder-time ceiling. North-star: **certified, active coaches**.

- **Trigger to start**: ~5 case studies + a repeatable, documented delivery process.
- **Recruit from**: independent business coaches, fractional operators, ex-corporate consultants who want a branded method (the ActionCOACH/EOS-implementer aspirant pool).
- **Offer**: Certification £4–7.5K + £1.2–2.4K/yr licence (per pricing analysis).
- **GTM for coaches**: content + the founder's growing authority + a "become a certified 1:90 coach" track on the site; later, a waitlist/cohort model to create scarcity.
- **The book** (if written in Phase 1) becomes the certification's credibility engine and the coaches' shared sales tool — exactly as *Traction* works for EOS.

## Phase 3 (Months 24+): Layer in software

- **Trigger**: enough clients + coaches actively running the method manually that they're *asking* for tooling.
- **GTM**: sell into the existing base (coaches deploy it to their clients) — warm, not cold. Software becomes a margin and retention layer, not an acquisition product.
