WHO IS THIS FOR

The 1:90 Align Coach Certification is designed for professionals who already understand business — and want a proprietary methodology to deepen their client impact and create predictable recurring income.

Business Coaches

If you currently work with business owners or leadership teams on strategy, growth, or performance, the 1:90 framework gives you a structured execution methodology to sit alongside your existing coaching approach. Clients get better results. You get a differentiator no other coach has.

Management Consultants

Traditional consulting engagements end when the report is delivered. The 1:90 system creates ongoing implementation work — and three income streams from a single client relationship. Consultancy fees, training referrals, and SaaS revenue share run concurrently throughout the engagement.

Operations Specialists

If your background is in operations, project delivery, or change management, you already understand execution. The certification gives you the strategic framework layer and the commercial model to package that expertise into a scalable practice rather than hourly consulting.

Fractional Executives

Fractional COOs, CEOs, and strategy directors who work across multiple client businesses find the 1:90 system gives them a consistent operating framework to deploy across their portfolio. Every client runs the same methodology, making your work more efficient and your results more consistent.

HOW IT WORKS

The certification is delivered through two intensive 1-2-1 sessions with a 1:90 Align master coach — not pre-recorded video modules. You learn the methodology by building a real implementation plan for a real client scenario.

01

Session 1 — Framework Mastery

Deep immersion in the Arc → Wave → Milestone → Task hierarchy. You work through 15 industry scenarios to understand how the framework applies across different business types, team sizes, and strategic contexts. By the end of Session 1, you can map any business situation to the correct framework level and diagnose Alignment Gaps on sight.

02

Software Configuration

Between sessions, you receive full access to the 1:90 Align platform and spend time configuring a sample client environment. This is not a demo — you build an actual Arc, create three Waves, set Milestones, assign Tasks, and run the dashboard. You leave knowing the software inside out, not from watching a tutorial but from using it.

03

Session 2 — Client Delivery

The second session covers client engagement: how to run an Arc planning session, how to facilitate a Wave Review, how to handle common objections, how to price your engagements across the three income streams, and how to use the facilitator kit in a live client environment. You leave certified and ready to take on your first client immediately.

04

Ongoing Access & Updates

Certification includes lifetime software access and all future platform updates. As 1:90 Align develops new features, scenarios, and facilitator materials, certified coaches receive them automatically. You're not buying a static course — you're joining a network of coaches running the same methodology with a commitment to continuous improvement.
