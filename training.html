IMPLEMENTATION TIMELINE

From initial enquiry to a fully operational 1:90 system running across your organisation typically takes four weeks. Most clients are executing their first Arc before the training programme officially concludes.

W1

Week 1 — Discovery & Setup

Initial scoping call, team size assessment, and platform configuration. Arc Leader training begins. The first Arc is drafted and the three-Wave structure is mapped. Team Leader training is scheduled for the following week.

W2

Week 2 — Leadership Alignment

Team Leader training runs. Arc is published to the organisation. Wave 1 Milestones are set by Team Leaders in the platform. Capacity plans are built. The first Ripple standup is facilitated live during training.

W3

Week 3 — Full Team Onboarding

Worker training delivered across the organisation. Tasks are created, linked to Milestones, and assigned. Daily Ripple standups begin. By the end of Week 3, every team member is using the platform and the Arc is live.

W4

Week 4 — First Wave Review

Facilitated Wave 1 Review session. Dashboard analysis with the Arc Leader. Wave 2 planning session completed. By Week 4, the system is running independently and the organisation is executing with complete line of sight from daily tasks to 90-day strategic goals.
